About Privacy Exposure Checker

🎯 Purpose

This tool helps you discover if your face appears on websites you didn't know about. In today's digital age, photos can spread across the internet without your knowledge. This service allows you to check if your face has been exposed online.

🔍 How It Works

  1. Database Building (Admin): We scrape public websites for images
  2. Face Detection: MTCNN detects faces in all images
  3. Embedding Generation: FaceNet creates unique face signatures
  4. Indexing: FAISS indexes all face embeddings for fast search
  5. User Upload: You upload your photo
  6. Comparison: We compare your face against our database using AI
  7. Results: You see where your face appears (if anywhere)

🛡️ Privacy & Security

What We DON'T Store:
  • ❌ Your uploaded photos
  • ❌ Your face embeddings
  • ❌ Your IP address or identity
  • ❌ Any search history
What We DO:
  • ✅ Process your photo in RAM only
  • ✅ Delete your photo immediately after search (within 6 seconds)
  • ✅ Only show you publicly available information
  • ✅ Run everything locally (no third-party APIs)

🔧 Technology Stack

  • MTCNN: Multi-task Cascaded Convolutional Networks for face detection
  • FaceNet: Deep learning model for face recognition
  • FAISS: Facebook AI Similarity Search for fast vector search
  • Flask: Python web framework
  • SQLite: Lightweight database for metadata

❓ FAQ

Q: Is this tool accurate?

The system uses industry-standard face recognition technology (FaceNet) with high accuracy. However, no system is 100% perfect.

Q: What if I find my face online?

You can contact the website owners to request removal, or use legal channels like DMCA takedown requests.

Q: How often is the database updated?

The administrator periodically scrapes new websites to keep the database current.

Q: Can I request a website to be added?

Contact the system administrator to suggest websites for inclusion in the database.

Q: Is my uploaded photo really deleted?

Yes! Your photo exists on the server for only ~6 seconds during processing, then is permanently deleted. We have no way to recover it.

⚠️ Disclaimer

This tool is for educational and privacy awareness purposes only. It only searches websites that have been specifically indexed by the administrator. The absence of results does not guarantee your face isn't elsewhere online. For comprehensive privacy protection, consider reverse image search services and privacy consultants.
